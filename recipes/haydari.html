Haydari (Turkish Yogurt Dip)

Haydari is an easy and fast prepared yogurt-based appetizer, which is also economical.Goes well with almost anything -traditionally- goes well with aniseed-flavored spirits (ouzo-raki) or kebab and pita bread but you can use it with almost any meat-dishes or as a garnish.

  • Prep time: 10 min
  • Cook time: none (0)
  • Servings: 2-4

Ingredients

  • 6 spoon strained yogurt
  • 1 pinch of dill
  • 1 spoon butter
  • 1 spoon dry mint
  • 1 spoon salt
  • 1 slice white cheese (you can use blue cheese too)

Guide to make strained yogurt from plain yogurt

Line a colander with two layers of cheesecloth and place over a medium bowl.

Place the yogurt on the cheesecloth and cover the colander with plastic wrap. Allow yogurt to drain 8 hours or overnight.

Directions

  1. First,heat the butter in the pan. At this stage, let's be careful not to turn on the stove on very high heat and burn the oil.
  2. Then add the mint, mix it for 1-2 turns and take it off the stove.
  3. Take the strained yogurt in the mixing bowl
  4. crush the white cheese with a fork, and finely chop the dill
  5. add crushed cheese and the chopped dill on the strained yogurt.
  6. Finally: add mint, butter, crushed garlic, salt and mix.
  7. take the Haydari appetizer on a serving plate and decorate it with olive oil and dill.
