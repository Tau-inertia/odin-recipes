Turksih Lentil Soup

A very well know traditional Turkish soup, nice and easy to make and you can cook it till there is enough water in the pot.

  • Prep time: 15min
  • Cook time: 30min
  • Serve: 6-8 people

Ingredients

  • Optional 1 celery or 1 carrot
  • one middle sized onion
  • one spoon of tomato paste
  • 2 glass of red lentil
  • one pinch of salt
  • 2L boiled water

Directions

  1. Chop 1 medium onion to the brunoise and fry it in olive oil.
  2. Then add washed red lentils with tomato paste.
  3. Optional (take it or move to 4th) While roasting our red lentils, we add 3-4 pieces of celery and carrots in the size of dice (but it's okay if you don't add it)
  4. After thoroughly roasting, add cold water (broth if available, otherwise it's okay, but we never add bouillon)
  5. After boiling our lentils in the pot, soup is ready to serve but you can use a blender before servings.
